Ethics According to Me - ESSAY 1

In every institution, whether it is a business, religious, educational or social institution, ethics will exist in some form of rules or guidelines for individuals. However, a frequently asked question would be, ‘What is ethics?’. Ethics can be defined from a professional view as a set of discipline concerns or principles which a person would often ask themselves; ‘What is morally good?’, ‘What is morally bad?’ and ‘What is the difference between morally right and wrong?’.
